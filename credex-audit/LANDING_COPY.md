## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no trial. The audit is free forever. We show you where you're overspending and how to fix it. That's it.

**Q: Do you store my spending data?**
A: We store your email if you choose to share it, and the tools and plans you entered — never your payment details. Your data is never sold.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price is sourced directly from official vendor pricing pages and verified weekly. Sources are cited in our pricing data file.

**Q: Why is this free? What's the catch?**
A: For teams with significant savings opportunities, we surface Credex — a marketplace for discounted AI credits. If you find value and want to save even more, that's how we make money. No obligation.

**Q: What if my tool isn't listed?**
A: We currently support Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. More tools are being added. Enter what you have and we'll audit those.
