# Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. A typical deal:
- Company spending $1,500/mo on AI tools
- Credex offers same credits at 20-30% discount
- Customer saves $300-450/mo
- Credex margin on the deal: ~15% of credit value = ~$225/mo per customer
- Annual contract value (ACV): ~$2,700/customer/year

Conservative estimate: **$2,000-3,000 ACV per converted customer.**

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel

| Channel | Cost | Conversions | CAC |
|---------|------|-------------|-----|
| Hacker News Show HN | $0 | 5-10 audits/post | $0 |
| Reddit posts | $0 | 3-8 audits/post | $0 |
| LinkedIn cold DM | $0 (time only) | 1-2/20 DMs | $0 |
| Newsletter mention | $0-200 | 10-30 audits | $0-20 |
| Existing Credex customers | $0 | High intent | $0 |

**Blended CAC at $0 paid budget: effectively $0 for first 6 months.**

Even if we value founder/eng time at $50/hr and spend 10 hrs/week on distribution:
- Monthly time cost: ~$2,000
- Monthly conversions at 2% of 500 audits: 10 customers
- CAC: $200/customer vs $2,700 ACV = **13x return**

## Conversion Funnel

| Stage | Number | Rate |
|-------|--------|------|
| Visits site | 1,000 | 100% |
| Completes audit | 400 | 40% |
| Submits email | 160 | 40% of audits |
| High savings (>$500/mo) | 48 | 30% of emails |
| Books Credex consultation | 10 | 20% of high savings |
| Purchases credits | 3-4 | 30-40% of consultations |

**From 1,000 visitors → 3-4 paying Credex customers**
**Revenue per 1,000 visitors: $6,000-12,000 ACV**

## What Makes This Profitable

The tool costs ~$0 to run at low scale:
- Vercel free tier: $0
- Supabase free tier: $0
- Anthropic API: ~$0.01 per summary = $4/mo at 400 audits/day
- Total monthly cost at 10k audits/mo: ~$50

Break-even: **1 paying Credex customer per month** covers all costs with 50x margin.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

**What would have to be true:**

- Average ACV: $2,500
- Customers needed: 400
- Monthly new customers needed: ~22/mo by month 18

**The math:**
- Month 1-3: 500 audits/mo → 2 customers/mo (ramp)
- Month 4-9: 2,000 audits/mo → 8 customers/mo (HN + Reddit flywheel)
- Month 10-18: 5,000 audits/mo → 22 customers/mo (newsletter + referral)

**Assumptions that must hold:**
1. Audit → consultation conversion stays above 15%
2. Consultation → purchase conversion stays above 30%
3. Credex can fulfill demand — supply of discounted credits scales
4. Average deal size stays above $2,000 ACV

**Biggest risk:** Supply constraint. Credex's model depends on companies that overforecast. If AI spending normalises and fewer credits are available at discount, the product promise weakens. Mitigation: diversify credit sources early.

$1M ARR in 18 months is aggressive but achievable if the HN launch gets traction and Credex's existing customer base seeds the first 50 conversions.
